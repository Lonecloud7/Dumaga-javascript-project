The Lightbringer series takes place in the Seven Satrapies. 
A world ruled by drafters using magic known as Chromaturgy. Drafting is the act of turning light into a physical substance, called Luxin. Drafters are revered, and respected in the town where they live, 
and are trained, known as The Chromeria.
